Back on the 23rd March, The Inquirer and The ABC reported that the ICANN (a non-profit organisation that governs IP address allocation and Top Level Domains) has authorised the .eu country-code top level domain (ccTLD). A thorough post at the Blog For Webmasters has all the details.This reminded me of a thought provoking piece that I read last year. Written by Tim Berners-Lee (pioneer of the internet, Knight and recipient of large sums of money), the article presents the dangers associated with handing out Top Level Domains left, right and center.
