Scoble, like everybody else, is peeved about blog-spam in search engine results and comment-spam on his blog.I'd say this is a prime opportunity for something like social filtering where the community gets control of whether a post will show up in blog search engine results or if a comment is worthy of being displayed. For comments, it would be pretty similar to what happens at slashdot.For spam blogs, once a certain number of users call bullshit on a given blog, its posts should no longer be returned in search results from technorati.It should be pretty simple for technorati to implement a Service that recieves spam brandings from users. A firefox plug-in that adds a right click/context menu option would suffice from the client end.There should also be a 'tolerance' level set when a user performs a search, allowing them to adjust the level of dissaproval needed before a blog is filtered out of the results.
