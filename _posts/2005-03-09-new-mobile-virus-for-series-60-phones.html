A new virus targeting the Symbian OS found on Nokia Series 60 mobiles has been discovered. Symantecs definition of SymbOS.Commwarrior.A has the following.

It attempts to spread using Multimedia Messaging Service (MMS) and Bluetooth as a randomly named .sis file.

According to an article at Mobile Tech, these Series 60 viruses aren't a big deal because its quite complicated to actually become infected and they don't do any real damage to your phone.
