--- layout: post title: Brittle author: dwight published: true date: 2005-09-07T23:45:00+0000 categories: - uncategorized ---

Said of software that is functional but easilybroken by changes in operating environment or configuration,or by any minor tweak to the software itself. Also, anysystem that responds inappropriately and disastrously toabnormal but expected external stimuli; e.g. a file systemthat is usually totally scrambled by a power failure is saidto be brittle....
