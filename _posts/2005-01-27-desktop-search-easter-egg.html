I doubt if any recent Microsoft releases contain real easter eggs but I've fond memories of playing the primative MS Excel Flight Simulator.Jon Udell gives the rundown & a screencast of what looks to me like an accidental easter egg; full-text searching via the Window's search tool.

Today's screencast is a 90-second short film that demonstrates Simon Burns' astonishing finding that desktop search has been right under our noses for years -- built into Windows but then crippled by weird design choices.
