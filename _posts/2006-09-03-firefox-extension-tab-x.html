One thing that kind of annoyed me when I switched from Opera to Firefox was that there is only a single 'close tab' button that's displayed off on its own on the right hand side of the tab bar. I much prefered having a close button on each tab.I've been on a bit of a firefox extension binge today and just installed Tab X which is takes care of my closee button gripe. It was created by Stephen Clavering who also has a few other extension that look neat.
