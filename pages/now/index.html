What am I working on

I have this project called Knownly.

It's a service that helps people publish websites without servers. With Knownly I'm looking to help more people create their first website by making it simple to write in design, build and host.

I'm also looking to show experienced designers and developers that many of the things they associate with "dynamic" websites can be achieved with a simple and elegant static HTML, CSS and Javascript. There is so much bloat and complexity in software and platforms like Wordpress, Wix and Squarespace. They're unnecessary for the sites you and I want to craft.
